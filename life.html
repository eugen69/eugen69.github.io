Game of Life

Locate beings on the field. Click to a being if you want to remove it. And play!

Sizing
Speed
0

Game of life is a Conway's cellular automaton. This is a zero-player game. You fill the field only in a first step. At each step in time:
- any being (it doesn't matter live or dead) will be live if it has 3 live neighbours.
- any live being with two live neighbours lives on to the next generation.
- any other cells become dead.
