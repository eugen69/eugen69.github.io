Bookkeeper


Android app for personal financial resources accounting.

Sometimes you think you depend on other people. They give you their kindness, or money or other resources. And you feel obliged to do things for them in exchange. But you really don't want to do it.

Try to express your relations in numbers. You will be amazed how many energy you spend for other people's wishes and how little it costs in money. So you can save your energy for your life. Money is earned easily. Your time and energy is much more precious. After that you can talk and act to people freely without feeling bound to them for they "attention" and "care".

For us has more in common with the clouds, or with the mountains, than with other people.

Bookkeeper serves:
- to express personal financial situation in numbers.
- to keep account of assets and liabilities.
Make instantly quantitative changes in balance (incomes and costs), add and remove items if you buy a new asset and etc.
Options:
- lock up by password;
- no ads, doesn't use internet connection for secure;
- user data backup;
