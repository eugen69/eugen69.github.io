Bookkeeper



Personal financial resources accounting

Bookkeeper serves:
- to express personal financial situation in numbers.
- to keep account of assets and liabilities.
Make instantly quantitative changes in balance
(incomes and costs), add and remove items
if you buy a new asset and etc).
Options:
- lock up by password;
- no ads, doesn't use internet connection for secure;
- user data backup;
